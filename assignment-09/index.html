How to Make Doritos Consumme

Recipe(?)

What You Need

  • Ingredients
    • 8 cups of Doritos, or about one large bag I will be using Cool Ranch
    • One half teaspoon of gelatin
    • 10 cups water, separated into 8 cups and 2 cups
  • Utensils
    • Cheese Cloth
    • Large pot
    • Colander
    • Large bowl

Step One

Get the groceries that you need. If you already have the gelatin... get help.

My husband and I go to Albertsons for basic food, but prefer produce from more local sources.

Thank god Doritos aren't produce and you don't need to buy organic for this.

Step Two


Boiling Water Stock photos by Vecteezy

Add 8 cups of water and the entire bag of Doritos in a large pot. Bring to a boil.

Step Three

  • Take your pan off the heat and let it steep for thirty minutes, like you are making Dorito tea. :)
    • After straining, discard the solids and return the water to the pot.
    • Bring water back to a boil.

Step Four

  • Combine the gelatin with the remaining two cups of water
  • Add to the Dorito tea

Step Five

  • Place the pot into the freezer until frozen solid.

Step Six

  • To unmold the Dorito ice, run the sides of the pot under hot water.
  • Once free, place the Dorito ice into a colander lined with two layers of cheese cloth.
    • Place the colander over a large bowl so that it can defrost at room temp.
    • This will probably take about a day.

Step Seven

  • When the ice is fully melted, discard the Dorito/gelatin scum.
  • The clear liquid that is left over is the final product!
