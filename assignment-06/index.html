Directions

  1. Crush chips using a rolling pin, a book, or whatever you have on hand
  2. Stir together crushed chips, sugar, and a pinch of sea salt in a sturdy pot
  3. Bring the nasty lil mixture to a boil. Stir until the sugar is dissolved
  4. Really show it to the mixture and smash it a few times with a potato masher
  5. Add the lemon (or lime) juice and continue to boil, stirring frequently
  6. Once the chip chunks float to the top, you're almost done!
  7. Mixture should stick to the spoon but fall off in clumps, around 10 to 12 minutes
  8. Skim the scum from the top; discard or eat if you're a freak
  9. Transfer your delicious Spicy Chip Jam into clean glass containers, leaving 3/4 inches of headroom
  10. Let the mixture cool completely then lid the containers
